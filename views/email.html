ServeLife Early Access Program!

Welcome! We are excited to have you join us on this epic adventure! You were selected to join ServeLife as one its founding contributors and work with us as we prepare to launch ServeLife to the world.

The ServeLife Early Access Program is just that - early access to our live, pre-launch environment. It is where we are actively building our global learning ecosystem. Thus you should expect frequent changes to the site and its functionality as our goal is to push new functionality, modifications and bug fixes as they become available each day.

Our hope and intention is that you will actively participate and engage throughout the early access program. So use the site's functionality. Yet keep in mind that what you contribute within the ecosystem will be visible once ServeLife is launched. So please don't use it as a test system.

We have engaged UserVoice to help us provide support and gain feedback throughout the early access program. You will see the UserVoice widget on the right middle of every page after login. If you encounter an issue or want to provide some feedback, click on the Feedback & Support tab and you will be taken to the ServeLife support site.

Please click here to activate your ServeLife membership.

Thank You Again for Joining! Also don't hesitate to contact either of us directly with questions, feedback or ideas!
